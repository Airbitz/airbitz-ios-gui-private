Request Bitcoin

Choose the destination wallet by clicking the top button next to [Wallet:]

To request bitcoin, simply tap the right arrow [>] button to proceed to the following step.

Another option is to enter the amount you would like to request. The amount can be entered in bitcoin or in fiat (USD, EUR). If no amount is entered, the Sender will determine the amount of bitcoin to send.

Tap the [Import] button and scan the QR code or enter the private key of the wallet to import. The import will sweep all the funds of the wallet and create a single deposit into your wallet.

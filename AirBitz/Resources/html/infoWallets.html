Your list of wallets appears on this screen.

Tap the green toggle bar to change between viewing fiat and Bitcoin values.

Create new wallets using the [+] button on the top left. Choose a fiat currency that you would like to track the value of your transactions in the wallet. Note that this fiat currency setting cannot be changed once set. You can change the global fiat currency for your account in the Settings tab in the lower right corner.

Reorder wallets by dragging the 6-dot icon on the right of each wallet. The top most wallet is your default wallet used for Send and Receive transactions.

Wallets may be archived by dragging them below the [Archive] header. Archived wallets do not show up in the balance in the top toggle bar. Archived wallets may be hidden by tapping the arrow to the right on the [Archive] header.

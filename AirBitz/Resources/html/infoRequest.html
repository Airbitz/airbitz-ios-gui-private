Request Bitcoin

Use the top most button to choose the wallet to have funds sent to for this particular request.

To request bitcoin from another person, simply tap the right arrow [>] button to proceed to the following step.

You can optionally enter the amount you would like to request. The amount can be entered in bitcoin or in fiat (USD, EUR). If no amount is entered, the Sender will determine the amount of bitcoin to send.

To import the private key/seed of another wallet, tap the [Import] button and scan the QR code of the wallet to import. The import will sweep all the funds of the wallet and create a single deposit into your wallet.

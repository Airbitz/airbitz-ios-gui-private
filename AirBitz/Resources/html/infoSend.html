Send Bitcoin

Send bitcoin from the wallet of your choice by clicking the top button next to [From].

Scan the QR code of another wallet to obtain the address of the wallet to send funds to.

Tap the Photo Album icon to choose a photo containing a QR code you would like to scan.

Manually type or paste the address of the destination wallet in the [To] field.

Tap the Bluetooth logo to select someone in your vicinity to pay via Bluetooth (BLE).

The [To] field also allows you to transfer bitcoin to another wallet in your account. Simply tap the destination wallet name in the dropdown that pops up.

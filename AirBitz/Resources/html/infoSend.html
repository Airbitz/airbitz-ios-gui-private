Send Bitcoin

Use the top most button to choose the wallet to send bitcoin from.

Scan the QR code of another wallet to obtain the address of the wallet to send funds to.

You can also tap the Photo Album icon to choose a photo containing a QR code you would like to scan.

You can also manually type or paste the address of the destination wallet in the [To] field.

The [To] field also allows you to create a transfer of bitcoin to another wallet in your account. Simply tap the destination wallet name in the dropdown that pops up.
